Tic Tac Watch

I love eating Tic Tacs. I would always have a pack in my soccer bag. I designed this so I will always have Tic Tacs on me when I get hungry. It uses a spring I found to act as an archimedes screw and push a platform to push into the Tic Tacs so they wouldn't shake around and make sound. I also made the watch 2 parts so you can replenish the Tic Tacs easier.
