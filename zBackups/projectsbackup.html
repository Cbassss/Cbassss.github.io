My Projects

Arduino sword

Inspired by a book called H.I.V.E. I read about a person that had this sword that pulsed this deep purple and I knew I had to make it. I used my CAD skills to create the frame that encases the arduino. I spent many iterations making it fit snugly in the container but still look good. I used my programming knowledge to program the arduino to pulse the RGB LED and turn off when I click a button on the top of the box.

FPV drone

I have always been interested in birds and their ability to fly. Once I saw a video of someone flying a cool shot I thought it was interesting, but once I saw that they were seeing it with VR like goggles, I knew I had to get one. I used my research skills to find out what components are the best and lightest(to not break any FAA rules). I used my CAD skills to create the frame and optimize it so that it was strong and light. A frame that i s 3D printed is way less stable than the standard 3” carbon fiber frames so I had to iterate and go through many failures to find something that worked. I have had many problems, Some causing me to lose or break my drone, but I am still iterating and trying to make the drone fly stable while still being 3D printed.

Bowl Clips

My parents wanted something to make sure their counter wouldn’t get scratched. They asked me to design something. After thinking for some time, I thought it would be fun to make little people hold it up, all with different faces or poses. I took measurements, then designed them in CAD and printed them. They turned out well and are being used.
